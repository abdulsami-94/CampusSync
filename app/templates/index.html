How it works

Simple by design.

Three steps. No bureaucracy. No black hole.

1

Student files a complaint

Pick a category, describe the issue, add a photo if needed. It takes less than a minute and goes directly to the right department.

2

Admin reviews & assigns

The dashboard surfaces complaints by urgency. Admins assign, update status, and communicate progress — all from one view.

3

Issue resolved. Record kept.

The student sees the resolution. The system logs it. The data gets better. Everyone moves on.
